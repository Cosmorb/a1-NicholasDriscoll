About Me
Engineer. Soldier. Builder.

I'm a senior Robotics Engineering (RBE) major at Worcester Polytechnic Institute, pursuing a minor in Philosophy, and on track to complete my undergraduate degree this C-term — with a master's to follow by Fall 2026.

My career focus is automation in advanced manufacturing. I bring a rare combination of engineering theory and genuine hands-on fabrication experience: before college I trained extensively on manual and CNC lathes and mills at a vocational school specializing in advanced manufacturing, and picked up CAD/CAM programming along the way.

I'm a proud member of the Baystate Battalion ROTC program and will soon commission into the Army National Guard. Outside academics I enjoy lifting, reading, and RPG video games — and I'm always tinkering with a new 3D printing and robotics build.
