About Me

I graduated from Worcester Polytechnic Institute with a B.S. in Robotics Engineering and a minor in Philosophy, and am completing my M.S. in Robotics Engineering (expected Fall 2026).

My focus is automation in advanced manufacturing. Before college I trained on manual and CNC lathes and mills at a vocational school specializing in advanced manufacturing, picking up CAD/CAM programming and metal fabrication along the way — skills that ground everything I build.

I commissioned as an officer into the Massachusetts Army National Guard through WPI's Baystate Battalion ROTC program. Outside the lab I enjoy lifting, reading, and tinkering with 3D printing and robotics builds.
